About Me

关于我

I am an undergraduate student at Yuanpei College, Peking University, currently working on AI for Science. My research focuses on molecular machine learning, computational metabolomics, and mass spectrometry.

I am particularly interested in integrating chemical knowledge, biological priors, and scientific reasoning into deep learning models, with the goal of building machine learning systems that better understand scientific data rather than relying solely on black-box learning.

Previously, I worked on 3D molecular generation and molecular representation learning. More recently, my research has shifted toward knowledge-guided molecular retrieval and metabolite identification from tandem mass spectra.
